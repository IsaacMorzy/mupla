  - body: |
      ## What to expect when you reach out

      Whether you fill out our contact form, send an email, or simply show up at an iftar, here is how we work the path from "I am interested" to "I am in":

      - **Within two business days**, you will hear from a real person (almost always one of our staff, sometimes a long-time volunteer) who will ask a few follow-up questions and point you at the right program.
      - **A short orientation**, 30 minutes, in person or on a call, so you know what mupla is, what it is not, and how the program you would join actually runs day to day.
      - **A first shift with an experienced volunteer**, nobody starts alone. You will spend your first few sessions with someone who has been doing the work long enough to answer any question.
      - **No pressure to commit forever.** You can try a program for a month, change your mind, or quietly step back. We do not chase people, and we would rather you stay because you want to than because we asked.
