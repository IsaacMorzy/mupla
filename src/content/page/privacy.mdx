      ## What we collect

      We collect the minimum information needed to serve you well. In practice, that means:

      - **Contact form messages.** When you use our contact form, we receive your name, email, and the message you wrote. We keep these in our email and records system so we can respond and follow up. We delete contact messages after 12 months unless they relate to an ongoing matter (a volunteer application, for example).

      - **Newsletter signups.** If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address in our email service (currently a third-party provider — see below). You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any newsletter email.

      - **Analytics.** We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand which pages on this site are most useful. We do not track individual visitors across other sites, and we do not sell any data — ever.

      - **Donation records.** When you donate, our payment processor (currently Stripe) handles the transaction. We receive your name, email, donation amount, and the fund you designated. We keep donation records for 7 years as required by law.

      ## Cookies and similar technologies

      We use a small number of cookies to make this site work:

      - **Essential cookies** for things like remembering your dark/light theme preference.
      - **Analytics cookies** (only with your consent, where required by law) to understand site usage.

      We do not use advertising cookies. We do not allow third-party tracking on this site.

      ## Third-party services

      We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate this site and our programs. Each has its own privacy practices:

      - **Email service** for newsletters and contact form messages.
      - **Payment processor** (Stripe) for donations.
      - **Hosting** (Vercel) for this website.
      - **Content management** (TinaCMS) for editing this site.

      We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone outside this list.

      ## Your rights

      You have the right to:

      - **Access** the personal information we hold about you.
      - **Correct** any information that is wrong.
      - **Delete** your information (subject to legal record-keeping requirements).
      - **Unsubscribe** from our newsletter at any time.
      - **Ask questions** about our privacy practices anytime.

      To exercise any of these rights, email us at **privacy@mupla.org**. We respond within 30 days.

      ## Changes to this policy

      We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the bottom and, for significant changes, notify subscribers by email.
