  A quiet monthly gathering for the last third of the night, the time our tradition teaches is when duas are most readily received. We pray two to eight rak'at together, then sit for tea and a short reflection until Fajr. All are welcome, no matter how much Arabic you know or how long it has been since you last prayed at night.

  The circle meets the third Saturday of every month at 1 a.m. (yes, that is the middle of the night, and yes, it is worth it). Questions? Email programs@mupla.org, or check the [events page](/events) for the next date.
