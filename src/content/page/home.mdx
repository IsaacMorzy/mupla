      ## Our mission

      The mupla foundation exists to put our values into practice. We believe a strong community is built,  slowly, patiently, in person,  through shared learning, mutual care, and the kind of service that doesn't always make the news.

      We are not a single project or a single program. We are the connective tissue between the people, mosques, schools, and families that make up our community. When someone needs help, we connect them. When someone wants to give, we point them at the work that matters.

      **Rooted in faith. Open to all. Accountable to our neighbors.**
