      ## What happens after you write

      Every message goes to our small staff team, who triage based on what you wrote. Most messages get a personal response within two business days. Here's what to expect for common requests:

      - **General questions** → a personal reply from a staff member within 48 hours.
      - **Volunteer applications** → we'll send you a short orientation form and connect you with a program lead.
      - **Donation questions** → our finance lead will follow up with a receipt and any details you need.
      - **Zakat requests** → our Zakat committee reviews every request confidentially; expect a response within 5 business days.
      - **Media inquiries** → routed to our communications lead; we aim to respond within 24 hours.

      Your information is never shared with anyone outside the mupla staff team. We respect your privacy.
