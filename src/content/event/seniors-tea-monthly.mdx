  A relaxed monthly afternoon for our elders, with tea, sweets, gentle conversation, and the company of neighbors. The third Tuesday of every month, 3 to 5 p.m.

  We pair newcomers with regulars so no one sits alone, and we keep the room quiet enough that even soft voices carry. Care aides are warmly welcome alongside the guests they accompany. If you know a neighbor who might enjoy this, bring them along. Questions? Reach us at programs@mupla.org, or see our [programs page](/programs) for the full monthly calendar.
