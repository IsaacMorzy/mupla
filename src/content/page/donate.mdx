      ## Where your donation goes

      We are committed to making every dollar count. In our last fiscal year, **90% of donations went directly to programs** — food, supplies, transportation, scholarships, and small stipends for the families we serve. The remaining 10% covered essential operations: rent, insurance, a small staff, and the technology that keeps us running.

      We accept:

      - **One-time or recurring general donations** — unrestricted support that lets us direct funds where they are most needed.
      - **Zakat** — 100% of which is distributed to eligible recipients according to the eight categories in the Qur'an (9:60).
      - **Sadaqah and Sadaqah Jariyah** — for ongoing programs and capital projects.
      - **In-kind donations** — groceries for the pantry, school supplies for youth programs, professional services.

      All donations are tax-deductible. You will receive an emailed receipt, and we publish an annual report every spring covering all funds received and spent.
