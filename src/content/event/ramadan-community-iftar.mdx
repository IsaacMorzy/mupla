  Every Friday during Ramadan, our doors open thirty minutes before Maghrib. A community-cooked halal dinner is on the table by sunset, followed by a short reflection, time to connect with neighbors, and plenty of food.

  We open the iftar to whoever walks through the door, longtime community members, first-time visitors, Muslim and non-Muslim friends. Childcare is provided on the night. If you'd like to help cook, set up, or clean afterward, write to **volunteer@mupla.org** and we'll match you with a kitchen shift.
